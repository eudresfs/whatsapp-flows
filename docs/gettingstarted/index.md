# Get Started

Source: https://developers.facebook.com/docs/whatsapp/flows/gettingstarted/

_Updated: 2025-10-28_

This section will help you get started with your very first (demo) Flow.

## Prerequisites

1. Set up [WhatsApp Business Platform](https://business.whatsapp.com/products/business-platform)
  - This will give you access to the [WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/partner_home/) as part of your WhatsApp Business Account.
2. [Set up the WhatsApp Business Platform Cloud API](/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/get-started)
  - Includes setup of Meta App and phone number
3. To be able to send Flows to your customers you will need to:
  - [Verify your business](/docs/development/release/business-verification)
  - Maintain a [high message quality](/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/messages/send-messages#message-quality).
